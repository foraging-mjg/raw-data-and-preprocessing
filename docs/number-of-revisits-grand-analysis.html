7.2 We replace structural missings with zero counts

We replace structural missings with zero: after all, this is equivalent with saying that they didn’t revisit any trees on their way to getting their first fruit, which is a true statement, even if it conceals that fact that they didn’t have any opportunities to revisit any trees on the way to getting their first fruit.
